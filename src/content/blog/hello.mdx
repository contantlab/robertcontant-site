I'm Robert Contant. I write here about the parts of cybersecurity I actually enjoy
practicing: networking, detection engineering, OSINT, the occasional AI-assisted
recon experiment, and the homelab that ties them together.

This site sits alongside [my GitHub](https://github.com/contantlab) on my resume.
If you're hiring for a hands-on engineer who is also literate in GRC, both halves
of that story should be visible from these pages.

## What's in scope

- Detection engineering notes from the homelab and from work I can write about safely
- OSINT tooling I build for myself (with the obligatory "do not point this at people you don't have permission to investigate" disclaimer)
- Short writeups when something surprises me — wins and losses both, since the losses are usually where the lesson is

## What's out of scope

Client work from [contantsolutions.com](https://contantsolutions.com), and anything
that would identify a specific environment without permission. That stuff stays
where it belongs.
